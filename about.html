Who We Are

Sky Dreams Travel is the parent company of Royal Hajj Umrah, based in Okara, Punjab. Since 2010, we have been the region's most trusted travel brand — helping thousands of families fulfil their dream of visiting Makkah Al-Mukarrama and Madinah Al-Munawwarah with complete peace of mind.

We believe every pilgrim deserves a journey that is not just smooth and affordable, but spiritually enriching. Our expert team handles every detail — from visa processing and flights to hotel bookings and ground transportation — so you can focus entirely on worship.
