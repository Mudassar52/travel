Your Journey Plan

14-Day Umrah Itinerary

A carefully planned day-by-day guide for our most popular Standard Package pilgrimage journey.

1 Day

Departure — Lahore to Jeddah

Lahore, Pakistan → Jeddah, KSA

Assemble at Allama Iqbal International Airport. Put on Ihram before boarding. Fly to King Abdulaziz International Airport, Jeddah. Transfer to Makkah on arrival.

  • Airport assembly & briefing at 6:00 AM
  • Ihram donned before departure
  • Direct transfer to hotel in Makkah
2 Day

Perform Umrah — Tawaf & Sa'i

Masjid Al-Haram, Makkah

Perform the complete Umrah — Tawaf around the Kaaba, Sa'i between Safa and Marwa, and Halq/Taqsir. Guided by our certified Umrah scholar.

  • Tawaf (7 circles around Kaaba)
  • Sa'i (7 rounds Safa to Marwa)
  • Halq / Taqsir (hair cut — exit Ihram)
3–8 Days

Worship & Ziyarat in Makkah

Makkah Al-Mukarrama

Six blessed days of worship, prayers at Masjid Al-Haram, and guided ziyarat of holy sites in and around Makkah. Ample time for personal ibadah and nafl Tawaf.

  • Prayers at Masjid Al-Haram (all 5 times)
  • Ziyarat: Jabal-e-Noor, Jabal-e-Thawr, Mina, Arafat
  • Muzdalifah, Jamarat, Hudaibiyah
  • Nafl Tawaf — unlimited times
9 Day

Transfer to Madinah Al-Munawwarah

Makkah → Madinah (4 hrs by road)

Comfortable AC coach transfer from Makkah to Madinah. Check in to hotel near Masjid-e-Nabawi. Evening visit to Roza-e-Rasool (PBUH).

  • Morning departure from Makkah
  • Lunch en-route included
  • First visit to Roza-e-Rasool (PBUH) in the evening
10–13 Days

Worship & Ziyarat in Madinah

Madinah Al-Munawwarah

Four spiritually enriching days in the City of the Prophet (PBUH). Prayers at Masjid-e-Nabawi, guided ziyarat of all important Islamic sites.

  • Masjid-e-Nabawi — all 5 prayers
  • Roza-e-Rasool (PBUH) — Salaam & Durood
  • Ziyarat: Masjid Quba, Masjid Qiblatayn, Uhud
  • Jannat-ul-Baqi, Dates Market
14 Day

Return Flight — Back to Pakistan

Madinah / Jeddah → Lahore

Final Fajr prayer at Masjid-e-Nabawi. Farewell to the holy city. Transfer to Madinah Airport or Jeddah for return flight to Lahore. Arrive home as a blessed pilgrim, Insha'Allah.

  • Farewell Tawaf (Wida) if returning from Makkah
  • Airport transfer — luggage handled by team
  • Arrive Lahore — welcome home!
